About - WeShop

WeShop is an e-commerce company headquartered in Bangalore, Karnataka, India, and registered in Singapore. The company initially focused on online book sales before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials, groceries, and lifestyle products.

Founder

WeShop was founded in December 2020 by Vikas, who is a college going student in Bhopal(Madhya pradesh). The company initially focused on online book sales with country-wide shipping

Achievements

To consumers, WeShop will be remembered as the company that created Indian e-commerce, that brought to the average Indian’s doorstep a wide range of affordable, quality products. The other is WeShop’s contribution in creating the startup ecosystem in India.

MarketPlace

WeShop Marketplace is India’s leading platform for selling online. Be it a manufacturer, vendor or supplier, simply sell your products online on WeShop and become a top e-commerce player with minimum investment.
